**Usage guidelines:**
- Use the full-colour logo on light/dark backgrounds where contrast allows
- Do not distort, stretch, or rotate the logo
- Maintain clear space equal to 10% of the logo width on all sides
- Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or effects unless specifically approved
